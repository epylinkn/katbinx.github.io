Their work and experiences have spanned from exploring the world of supply chains, working in a domestic robotics company in China, designing the perfect farm, assisting in humanitarian relief airlifts, and building labyrinths with a new age cult in their youth.

They are currently working for the Toronto Youth Food Policy Council as Education Lead and working with Social Body Labs as a research assistant.
